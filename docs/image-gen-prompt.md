## Detail Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of the signature rotating bowl stack: overlapping glass rims on a horizontal spindle with soft non-marring supports and collars, a wetted fingertip lightly touching one rim, tiny water droplets controlled by a tray below, polished hardware and warm wood frame blurred in the background, crisp glass reflections, soft raking light, high detail, no measurements.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, wine-glass table setup, broken glass, unsafe exposed belts, dimension callouts, impossible intersecting bowls, extra fingers, or electrical motor-only drive replacing the treadle concept.
