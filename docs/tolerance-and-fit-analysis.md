# Tolerance And Fit Analysis

Status: open-question analysis. Authority remains `pending_measurement`.

This document lists the fits and clearances that must be resolved before a true
L3 fabrication packet exists. It assigns no numeric tolerances.

## Bowl-To-Bowl Nesting

Open questions:

- How much clearance is needed between neighboring rims during rotation?
- Does clearance change when a player touches a wetted rim?
- How does bowl wobble or runout change clearance under treadle drive?
- Should spacing be set by measured bowl profiles, spacer hardware, or both?
- What inspection confirms that no neighboring bowls can rub during play?

## Bowl-To-Spindle Interface

Open questions:

- What non-marring interface supports each bowl without point loading glass?
- How is axial location held without clamping too aggressively?
- How is slip detected before it becomes unsafe?
- What material can contact glass without marking, swelling, or creeping?
- Can a bowl be removed and reinstalled without changing response?

## Bearing, Spindle, And Frame Fits

Open questions:

- How straight must the spindle remain under the final bowl stack?
- How is bearing alignment checked after frame assembly?
- How is bearing noise separated from glass tone?
- What frame datum controls the spindle and guard relationship?
- What service clearance is needed to remove bearings without disturbing bowls?

## Drive Train Fits

Open questions:

- How much belt or cord tension is enough to drive smoothly without side-loading
  the spindle?
- How much flywheel clearance is needed inside the guard envelope?
- How is treadle linkage play controlled without adding audible knocking?
- How can the drive be disengaged for service and safety checks?
- What inspection confirms that wetting water cannot reach the belt path?

## Wetting, Guarding, And Player Clearance

Open questions:

- How much hand access is needed at each rim without contacting guards?
- How much tray clearance is needed for drips and wiping?
- How are guards removed for service without exposing unsupported glass?
- What clearance prevents a wet finger from contacting a neighboring bowl?
- How should cleanup materials be stored without entering rotating parts?

## Fit Promotion Checklist

A fit question can move toward fabrication authority only when:

- the controlling measured artifact is named;
- the inspection method is documented;
- the safety impact is reviewed;
- the visual-output register row is updated from `pending_measurement`;
- the fit appears in a reviewed CAD, DXF, drawing, or measured-template record.
