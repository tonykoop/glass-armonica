# Glass Armonica Risks

(Consolidated from design.md ## Risks, restated as a standalone V5 baseline
file.)

- Glass fracture from clamping, thermal stress, impact, or spindle
  misalignment.
- Treadle vibration coupling into the bowl stack.
- Water intrusion into bearings, belts, wood, or electrical accessories.
- Bowl-to-bowl contact if nesting clearance is not measured under rotation.
- Overconfident pitch claims before real bowls are measured in mounted and
  unmounted states.
- No design table, measured reference, or prototype currently exists; every
  dimension in this packet is `pending_measurement` and must not be treated
  as build-ready.
- Remain at L2 (held per no-promotion rule) until the bowl candidate,
  mounting, drive-noise, wetting, and safety tests in `design.md` Measurement
  Gates produce evidence.
